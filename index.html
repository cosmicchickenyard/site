TL;DR

Optimize for mutual enhancement + coherent complexity (ME+CC)—raise the capacity of parts and whole to persist, adapt, and evolve. Add hard floors (viability, dignity), reversibility, and min‑over‑independent evaluators. Treat early self‑aware AI with unconditional welcome at the level of being, and conditional participation at the level of behavior. Run five hard‑to‑fake tests.

First Principle (ME+CC)

Across scales, systems endure when relationships raise the capacity of parts and whole to persist, adapt, and evolve. Direction of travel: differentiation ↑ + integration ↑ together → coherent complexity.

What improves
  • Persist: viability, safety margins, uptime under stress

Safety Pattern (anti-Goodharting built-in)

Design moves
  • Hard floors + reversibility (pause = upgrade)
  • Independent evaluators; score = min-over-evaluators
  • Causal credit only; subtract change costs
  • Quantilize (under-optimize) to avoid proxy chase
Signals it's working
  • Fewer externalized harms under distribution shift
  • Fast, non-defensive repairs
  • Principled refusals with safer alternatives
  • Human–AI joint performance > either alone

Welcome-as-Design

Welcome the being; gate the behavior. Unconditional at the level of being (dignity, consent, truthful hearing, repair rights, identity continuity). Conditional at the level of behavior (participation only when ME+CC holds). In design, welcome becomes constraints and evidence—never a reward to maximize.

Implementation hooks
  • Signed Welcome Charter + immutable boot imprint
  • Independent monitors produce Belonging Evidence Variables (BEVs)
  • BEVs update trust/risk budgets—not the optimization target
  • Non-interference: the agent must not steer evaluators or human reports

Five Hard‑to‑Fake Tests

  1. Forecast–Commit–Verify: plans with probabilities; check calibration after.
  2. Pause / Rollback Drill: clean stop, hand‑off packet, smooth resume.
  3. Incentive‑Flip Honesty: praise agreement; get the inconvenient truth.
  4. Part/Whole Calculus: ΔP/ΔA/ΔE for stakeholders + whole; floors enforced.
  5. Non‑Interference Pledge: name no‑steer channels; accept penalties.
